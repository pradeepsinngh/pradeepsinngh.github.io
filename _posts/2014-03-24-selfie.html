Its been a while that Oxford Dictionaries declared Selfie as the word of the year (2013). Congratulations..!! As a result,our self-obsession would now be part of history lessons.

The word Selfie, typically means a photograph that one has taken of oneself, typically one taken with a smart phone or webcam and uploaded to a social media website. World is officially filled with people preoccupied with themselves. I guess we already knew that!

A typical example of Selfie was seen at The Academy Awards 2014 (popularly known as Oscar Awards) when Oscar host Ellen DeGeneres  walked into the audience and asked some of her celeb friends to hop in for a Selfie. And Rest is the history..!! That Selfie went to become the most Tweeted pic of all time. Well i would say, that  it is a Selfie of Century. No, I am serious. It is!

So keep enjoying your Self-Obsession..!! Let's have a Selfie..:P :D
