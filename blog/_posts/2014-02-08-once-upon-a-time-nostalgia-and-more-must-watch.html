      This is the tenth time I am watching the "Google search :reunion’ ad and still it manages to make me shed a tear with a smile on my face.The story-telling set in the times of partition and what people went through as they got  separated from  their place and people of origin gets brilliantly reflected in the advertisement.The story is narrated by a grandfather and a historical event is almost witnessed and understood by the granddaughter.  Since the ad has gone viral and the entire world has seen it, I will not further describe it and rather would ask you to watch it if u haven’t.

Forget for a moment that the video you’re about to watch is an advertisement. Think of it instead as a short film, and just watch. The video runs only 3 minutes, 32 seconds. If you’re not in tears, or at least misty, when it ends, then you have no soul.

The gist is this: a man in Delhi tells his granddaughter about his childhood friend, Yusuf. He hasn’t seen Yusuf since the Partition of India in 1947, when India and Pakistan became separate countries and the two friends were forced to separate. The man’s granddaughter arranges for the two to meet again.
