   My absence from my own blog has to go down as one of the greatest examples of procrastination. I always kept searching for a topic to write but that is no reason.

But I promise that from now on ya’ll will be hearing more from me. The sole reason being University Semester exams. Mumbai University’s semester exams are a month long (literally) and with the summer break for other students, you often wonder as to how did you end up in the Engineering field.

All thoughts related to the ongoing Semester exams lead to the ‘Why did I opt for Engineering?’ thought.

As it happened today, I woke to learn that my folks weren’t at home. I was free to do whatever I wanted. Put an Elvis track on the System, poured a cup of coffee and grabbed a slice of the leftover pizza. Leftover Pizza is the best thing that ever happened to mankind since the invention of wheel.

So I was having a blast and just then I realized that tomorrow I have an exam for which I hadn’t even started preparing. And I eventually ended up with the 'Why did I opt for Engineering?' thought.

Gotta go now, study a bit. Ya'll know how University exams are. See ya'll soon.
