As and When I make my self comfortable some where, Life throws an exception at me.

I wonder Why? Why at me? .

I don't believe in God, so there is no way that almight is planning something good for me.

This time the exception is rather good than bad. At least it seems to be. I'm still to figure out this. May be.. time will tell.

I joined Raman Research Institute, Bangalore in July (2015). And as part of my job here I was supposed to work here for a year. I was slected after my sucessful run as an Research Intern here in Dec 2014 -Feb 2015.
Getting this opportunity to work in highly competitve research environment with state of the art technology and under the guidance of some of the most smartest/intelligent people in India (or may be in the World), I bounced on it & with out giving any second thought I accepted the offer.

I was working in Digital Signal Processing Lab in Electronics Engineering Group and all 3 projects that I was supposed to work dealt with FPGA, Signal Processing & Parallel Programmimg.

But within some months, came an exception (rather an opportunity).

So, One day out of the blue...I got an email form one of the Prof. from IIT Bombay and email read like this :

Pradeep, I like your CV and I would like to interview you for an exciting project in collabration with Space Application Center, ISRO, Ahemdabad.

I didn't replied to his e-mail for 3 days. I was quite intimidated. I mean this is proper research. But, having published some research papers earlier & been working in research environment already for quite a some time, I was confident.

And, in next couple of days my interview was fixed (Skype).

I was excited (and nervous too). Then, came the D-Day.

And, guess What?
Interview just turned out to be an mere discussion about my past projects, experience, future plans & some questions about Signal & Image Processing.
Phhheeeww. I was happy. I thought, I made it. Big ISRO dream. :D

And, then suddenly Prof. said..You have to come down to Mumbai (IIT-B) to meet me in person. For what..?
Well, for a full fledged interview..?
I was nervous again but ready indeed. Meh.

Then, dates were decided. I was asekd to come on 4th Jan 2016
(My New Year...started with an Interview :()

But, this time this exception really turned out to be good. I cleared everything and was selcted as Research Assistant.
YAY!
Icing on the cake was my (our) collabration with Space Application Centre, ISRO, Ahmedabad.

So, finally..I have joined my new job. I'm working at IIT Bombay and funded by ISRO. :D

Life changing possibilities are exciting. Peace. :D
